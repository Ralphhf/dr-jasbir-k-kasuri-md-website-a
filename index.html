About Dr. Kasuri

With over two decades of dedicated service in family medicine, Dr. Jasbir K. Kasuri has established herself as a trusted healthcare partner in Edison, New Jersey. Her approach combines cutting-edge medical expertise with genuine compassion, ensuring each patient receives personalized care tailored to their unique needs.

Dr. Kasuri believes in building lasting relationships with her patients, taking the time to understand not just their symptoms, but their lifestyle, concerns, and health goals. This comprehensive approach has earned her the trust and loyalty of countless families throughout the community.
